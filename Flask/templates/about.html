What We Do

We aspire to become an investment-advisory group that incorporates state-of-the-art financial planning, investment portfolio management and a number of aggregated financial services; similar to offerings by hedge fund managers and big banks—usually out of reach by the average retail investor.

While it’s impossible to know for anyone to know the future absolutely, there is wisdom in trying to peak into future possibilities. In the pursuit of wiser investing we aim to discover which sector will have the best likelihood of being a top performer each earnings season. To showcase our skill, we offer for free, one analytical methodology that can put the odds in your favor for determining which sectors will be the strongest and which sectors will be the weakest for the coming earning season: a form of relative strength analysis.

When conducting a relative strength analysis, analysts compare the strength of each stock market sector against every other sector by determining the percentage growth/contraction each sector has experienced since a specific start date in the past. We will modify this approach for simplicity but keep the spirit intact. Then we will employ a bit of physics, Newton’s first law of motion — an object in motion tends to stay in motion — and assume that, barring any unforeseen events or information, the sectors that have been performing well will continue to perform well and the sectors that have been performing poorly will continue to perform poorly this season.
